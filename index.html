About the Firm

Gaurav Saini & Associates is a New Delhi based law firm providing litigation, advisory and compliance related legal services. The firm operates strictly in accordance with applicable laws and professional ethics prescribed by the Bar Council of India.

Practice Areas

Civil & Criminal Litigation Labour & Service Law Corporate & Commercial Advisory Tax, Risk & Regulatory Compliance Intellectual Property Rights Real Estate & Property Law Arbitration & Mediation Legal Drafting & Opinions

Civil & Criminal Litigation

The firm represents clients before courts and tribunals in civil and criminal matters including contractual disputes, property litigation, recovery proceedings and injunction matters. Criminal practice includes representation in complaints, FIR related matters, bail proceedings and trial proceedings. Clients are assisted at various stages including drafting, filing, hearings and appeals. All litigation work is undertaken in accordance with applicable procedural laws and professional ethics.

Labour & Service Law

The firm advises individuals and organizations on employment related legal matters including industrial disputes, disciplinary proceedings and termination issues. Clients may be assisted in labour law compliance and workplace regulatory matters. Representation may be provided before labour courts and tribunals where required.

Corporate & Commercial Advisory

The firm provides legal advisory services relating to commercial transactions and corporate documentation. Assistance may include drafting and reviewing commercial agreements and contracts. Businesses may be advised on regulatory obligations and corporate governance issues.

Tax, Risk & Regulatory Compliance

The firm provides advisory services relating to regulatory compliance and taxation matters. Clients may be assisted in understanding statutory compliance obligations and regulatory frameworks applicable to businesses.

Intellectual Property Rights

The firm assists clients in matters relating to protection and enforcement of intellectual property rights including trademarks and copyrights. Assistance may include registration procedures and documentation.

Real Estate & Property Law

The firm advises clients on property transactions and disputes relating to property rights including title verification and documentation. Representation may be provided in property related disputes before competent authorities.

Arbitration & Mediation

The firm assists clients in resolving disputes through arbitration and mediation mechanisms including settlement discussions and arbitration proceedings.

Legal Drafting & Opinions

The firm undertakes drafting of legal documents including agreements, contracts, pleadings and legal notices. Legal opinions may be provided based on the facts and applicable law.
