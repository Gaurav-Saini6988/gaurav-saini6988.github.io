Practice Area Details

Civil & Criminal Litigation

The firm represents clients before courts and tribunals in civil and criminal matters including contractual disputes, property litigation and recovery proceedings. Clients are assisted in drafting pleadings, filing matters and court appearances. Criminal practice includes advisory relating to complaints, FIR related matters and bail applications. Representation may also be provided during trial proceedings and appeals. All litigation services are rendered in accordance with applicable procedural laws.

Labour & Service Law

The firm advises individuals and organizations in employment related matters including industrial disputes and service issues. Assistance may include advisory relating to employment contracts and workplace compliance. Clients may also be represented before labour courts and tribunals. The firm assists in disciplinary proceedings and employment disputes. All services are provided in accordance with applicable labour legislation.

Corporate & Commercial Advisory

The firm provides legal advisory services relating to commercial transactions and corporate governance. Assistance may include drafting and reviewing commercial agreements and contracts. Businesses may be advised on regulatory compliance and corporate documentation. Legal risks associated with business transactions are also reviewed. Services are provided within the framework of corporate and commercial laws.

Tax & Regulatory Compliance

Advisory services are provided relating to statutory compliance and regulatory frameworks applicable to businesses. Clients may be assisted in understanding tax related legal issues and regulatory obligations. The firm assists in documentation and legal compliance matters. Guidance may also be provided in addressing regulatory proceedings. Services are rendered in accordance with applicable tax and regulatory laws.

Intellectual Property Rights

The firm assists clients in matters relating to protection and enforcement of intellectual property rights. Services include advisory relating to trademarks, copyrights and other IP assets. Clients may be assisted with registration procedures and documentation. The firm also assists in addressing intellectual property disputes where required. Services are rendered within the framework of applicable intellectual property laws.

Real Estate & Property Law

Legal advisory services are provided relating to property transactions and disputes. Assistance may include title verification, property documentation and regulatory compliance. Clients may be assisted in property litigation and dispute resolution. The firm provides guidance in understanding legal aspects of property ownership and transfers. Services are provided in accordance with applicable property laws.

Arbitration & Mediation

The firm assists clients in resolving disputes through arbitration and mediation mechanisms. Advisory services may be provided during arbitration proceedings and settlement discussions. Assistance may include drafting arbitration related documentation. Clients may also be represented during arbitration proceedings where appropriate. The objective remains efficient and lawful dispute resolution.

Legal Drafting & Opinions

The firm undertakes drafting of legal documents including agreements, contracts, pleadings and notices. Legal opinions may be provided based on the facts and applicable law. Drafting services emphasize clarity and legal accuracy. Clients may be assisted in preparing documentation for litigation or transactions. Legal opinions are advisory in nature and based on applicable legal provisions.
