About the Firm

Providing litigation, advisory and compliance-focused legal services to individuals, businesses and institutions across diverse areas of law.

Gaurav Saini & Associates is a New Delhi-based law firm providing litigation, advisory, and compliance-related legal services to individuals, businesses, and institutions. The firm handles matters across diverse areas of law including civil and criminal litigation, corporate and commercial advisory, taxation, regulatory compliance, intellectual property, and real estate matters.

The firm adopts a professional and methodical approach to legal practice with emphasis on careful legal analysis, precise documentation, and effective representation before courts and other competent authorities. Gaurav Saini & Associates operates strictly in accordance with the professional standards and ethical rules prescribed by the Bar Council of India.

Individual Clients

Civil & Criminal Litigation

The firm represents clients before courts and tribunals in civil and criminal matters including contractual disputes, recovery proceedings, property disputes, injunctions, and declaratory reliefs. Assistance is provided at various stages of litigation including case assessment, drafting of pleadings, filing of proceedings, and representation before competent courts. In criminal matters, the firm advises and represents clients in complaints, FIR-related issues, anticipatory and regular bail applications, trial proceedings, and appellate remedies. The objective is to provide structured legal representation while ensuring adherence to procedural and substantive legal requirements.

Labour & Service Law

The firm advises individuals and organizations on employment and service-related legal matters including industrial disputes, disciplinary proceedings, workplace policies, and termination-related issues. Assistance may be provided in the drafting and review of employment agreements, service contracts, and internal compliance documentation. Where required, representation may also be undertaken before labour courts, tribunals, and other competent authorities in relation to employment disputes and service-related proceedings.

Corporate & Commercial Advisory

The firm provides legal advisory services to businesses in relation to corporate governance, commercial transactions, and contractual arrangements. Assistance may include drafting, reviewing, and negotiating commercial agreements, partnership arrangements, service agreements, and other business documentation. Clients may also be advised on regulatory compliance requirements applicable to their business operations, with the objective of ensuring legally sound and commercially practical documentation.

Tax, Risk & Regulatory Compliance

The firm provides advisory services relating to statutory compliance and regulatory frameworks applicable to businesses and individuals. Clients may be assisted in understanding their obligations under applicable taxation and regulatory laws. Advisory services may include guidance on compliance procedures, regulatory documentation, and risk assessment in business operations. Where necessary, assistance may also be provided in responding to regulatory proceedings and compliance-related matters.

Intellectual Property Rights

The firm advises clients on matters relating to the protection and management of intellectual property including trademarks, copyrights, and related proprietary rights. Assistance may include guidance on registration procedures, documentation, and protection of intellectual property assets. Clients may also be advised on issues relating to infringement and protection of brand identity in accordance with applicable intellectual property laws.

Real Estate & Property Law

The firm advises clients on legal aspects of property transactions and property-related disputes. Assistance may include title verification, review of property documentation, and advisory relating to regulatory requirements governing property ownership and transfers. Clients may also be assisted in drafting property agreements and addressing disputes relating to property rights before competent courts and authorities where required.

Arbitration & Mediation

The firm assists clients in resolving disputes through alternative dispute resolution mechanisms including arbitration and mediation. Advisory services may include review of arbitration clauses, preparation of arbitration documentation, and participation in arbitration proceedings where appropriate. The firm also assists clients during settlement discussions and mediation processes with the objective of facilitating structured and legally compliant dispute resolution.

Legal Drafting & Opinions

The firm undertakes drafting of legal documents including agreements, contracts, notices, pleadings, and other legal instruments required in business transactions and litigation matters. Legal opinions may also be provided based on the facts presented and the applicable legal framework. The drafting process emphasizes clarity, legal accuracy, and enforceability of contractual terms while ensuring that documentation aligns with applicable statutory provisions.
