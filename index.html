About the Firm

Gaurav Saini & Associates is a New Delhi based law firm providing litigation, advisory and compliance related legal services. The firm operates strictly in accordance with applicable laws and professional ethics prescribed by the Bar Council of India.

Practice Areas

Civil & Criminal Litigation Labour & Service Law Corporate & Commercial Advisory Tax, Risk & Regulatory Compliance Intellectual Property Rights Real Estate & Property Law Arbitration & Mediation Legal Drafting & Opinions

Civil & Criminal Litigation

The firm represents clients before courts and tribunals in civil and criminal matters, subject to applicable procedural and substantive laws.

Civil litigation includes disputes relating to contracts, property, recovery, injunctions and declaratory reliefs.

Criminal practice involves advisory and representation in complaints, FIR-related matters, bail applications, trials and appellate proceedings.

All litigation services are rendered strictly within the framework of law and professional responsibility.

Labour & Service Law

The firm advises and represents individuals and organizations in matters arising out of employment and service conditions.

This includes industrial disputes, disciplinary proceedings, termination related matters and service jurisprudence.

Advisory services are also provided on compliance with labour laws and employment regulations.

Representation is subject to jurisdiction and applicable statutory provisions.

Corporate & Commercial Advisory

Corporate advisory services are provided in relation to commercial transactions, agreements and corporate governance.

The firm assists in contract drafting, review, interpretation and dispute management.

Advisory services are rendered in accordance with corporate and commercial laws in force.

The focus remains on lawful compliance and risk mitigation.

Tax, Risk & Regulatory Compliance

The firm provides advisory in matters relating to direct and indirect taxation, subject to prevailing laws.

Services include compliance advisory, regulatory risk assessment and representation before authorities where permissible.

Clients are assisted in understanding statutory obligations and compliance frameworks.

All tax advisory is strictly non-promotional and law-based.

Intellectual Property Rights

The firm provides legal services in relation to trademarks, copyrights and other intellectual property rights.

Assistance is provided in protection, enforcement and dispute resolution relating to IP assets.

Advisory is rendered in accordance with applicable intellectual property laws.

Services are provided strictly within statutory and regulatory frameworks.

Real Estate & Property Law

The firm advises clients on property related transactions and disputes.

This includes title due diligence, documentation and property litigation.

Clients are assisted in navigating compliance and regulatory requirements.

All services are subject to applicable real estate and property laws.

Arbitration & Mediation

The firm assists clients in alternative dispute resolution mechanisms including arbitration and mediation.

Support is provided during proceedings, settlement discussions and enforcement processes.

ADR services are rendered in accordance with statutory frameworks.

The objective remains lawful and efficient dispute resolution.

Legal Drafting & Opinions

The firm undertakes drafting of legal documents, pleadings and agreements.

Legal opinions are prepared based on facts and applicable law.

Drafting services emphasize clarity, compliance and legal accuracy.

Opinions are advisory in nature and non-binding.
