About the Firm

Gaurav Saini & Associates is a New Delhi based law firm providing litigation, advisory and compliance related legal services. The firm operates strictly in accordance with applicable laws and professional ethics prescribed by the Bar Council of India.

Practice Areas

Civil & Criminal Litigation Labour & Service Law Corporate & Commercial Advisory Tax, Risk & Regulatory Compliance Intellectual Property Rights Real Estate & Property Law Arbitration & Mediation Legal Drafting & Opinions

Civil & Criminal Litigation

The firm represents clients before courts and tribunals in civil and criminal matters including contractual disputes, recovery proceedings, property disputes, injunctions and declaratory reliefs. The firm assists clients at every stage of litigation including case evaluation, drafting of pleadings, filing of proceedings and representation before competent courts. Criminal practice includes advisory and representation in complaints, FIR related matters, anticipatory and regular bail applications, trial proceedings and appeals. The objective is to provide strategic legal representation while ensuring compliance with procedural and substantive laws.

Labour & Service Law

The firm advises individuals and organizations on employment and service related legal matters including industrial disputes, disciplinary proceedings, wrongful termination and employment documentation. Clients may be assisted in drafting employment contracts, service agreements and workplace policies. Advisory services may also include compliance with applicable labour legislation and regulatory obligations. Where required, representation may be provided before labour courts, tribunals and other competent authorities in employment related disputes.

Corporate & Commercial Advisory

The firm provides legal advisory services to businesses in relation to commercial transactions, corporate governance and contractual matters. Assistance may include drafting, reviewing and negotiating commercial agreements, partnership arrangements, service agreements and other business documentation. Clients may also be advised on regulatory compliance requirements applicable to their industry. The firm focuses on minimizing legal risk while ensuring that business transactions remain legally sound and commercially practical.

Tax, Risk & Regulatory Compliance

The firm provides advisory services relating to statutory compliance and regulatory frameworks applicable to businesses and individuals. Clients may be assisted in understanding their legal obligations under applicable taxation and regulatory laws. Advisory services may include guidance on compliance procedures, regulatory documentation and risk assessment in business operations. The firm also assists in addressing regulatory proceedings where required and ensuring compliance with applicable legal provisions.

Intellectual Property Rights

The firm assists clients in matters relating to the protection and enforcement of intellectual property rights including trademarks, copyrights and related proprietary rights. Services may include advisory relating to registration procedures, documentation and protection of intellectual property assets. Clients may also be advised in relation to infringement related issues and protection of brand identity. The objective is to safeguard intellectual property assets while ensuring compliance with applicable intellectual property laws.

Real Estate & Property Law

The firm advises clients on legal aspects of property transactions and property related disputes. Assistance may include title verification, review of property documentation and advisory relating to regulatory requirements applicable to property ownership and transfers. Clients may also be assisted in drafting property agreements and addressing disputes relating to property rights. Representation may be provided before competent courts and authorities where required.

Arbitration & Mediation

The firm assists clients in resolving disputes through alternative dispute resolution mechanisms including arbitration and mediation. Advisory services may include review of arbitration clauses, preparation of arbitration documentation and representation during arbitration proceedings where appropriate. The firm also assists clients during settlement discussions and mediation processes with the objective of achieving efficient and legally compliant dispute resolution.

Legal Drafting & Opinions

The firm undertakes drafting of legal documents including agreements, contracts, notices, pleadings and other legal instruments required in business transactions and litigation matters. Legal opinions may also be provided based on the facts presented and the applicable legal framework. The drafting process focuses on clarity, legal accuracy and enforceability of contractual terms. Clients may be assisted in preparing documentation necessary for litigation, compliance and commercial transactions.
