Gaurav Saini & Associates is a full-service law firm providing high-quality legal services with a strong focus on integrity, efficiency and client success.

Gaurav Saini – Advocate & Legal Consultant

Gaurav Saini is an Advocate and Legal Consultant based in New Delhi, advising individuals, businesses, and international clients on a wide range of legal matters. He is known for his strategic, detail-driven and client-focused approach to legal practice.

With experience across civil, criminal, labour, and advisory matters, Gaurav provides practical and commercially sound legal solutions rather than just theoretical advice. His work includes drafting and vetting contracts, legal opinions, compliance support, dispute resolution, and court representation.

He regularly assists foreign law firms, overseas clients, and corporate entities in understanding and navigating Indian laws, regulations, and procedural requirements. His ability to translate complex Indian legal issues into clear, actionable guidance makes him a reliable legal partner for cross-border matters.

Gaurav believes that effective legal service is built on transparency, communication, and precision. Every client receives personal attention, timely updates, and well-researched legal strategy aimed at protecting their rights and advancing their interests.
