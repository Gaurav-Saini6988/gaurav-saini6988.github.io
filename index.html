About the Firm

Gaurav Saini & Associates is a New Delhi based law firm providing litigation, advisory and compliance related legal services. The firm operates strictly in accordance with applicable laws and professional ethics prescribed by the Bar Council of India.

Practice Area Details

Civil & Criminal Litigation

The firm represents clients before courts and tribunals in civil and criminal disputes including contractual disputes, recovery proceedings, property disputes and injunction matters.

Labour & Service Law

The firm advises individuals and organizations on employment matters including industrial disputes, service issues and workplace regulatory compliance.

Corporate & Commercial Advisory

Legal advisory services relating to commercial transactions, corporate documentation and business regulatory compliance.

Tax & Regulatory Compliance

Advisory services relating to regulatory frameworks and statutory compliance obligations applicable to businesses.

Intellectual Property Rights

Advisory and assistance relating to trademarks, copyrights and protection of intellectual property assets.

Real Estate & Property Law

Advisory services relating to property transactions, title verification and property disputes.

Arbitration & Mediation

Representation and advisory relating to arbitration proceedings and alternative dispute resolution mechanisms.

Legal Drafting & Opinions
